Accessibility Statement

WinThrive is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, regardless of ability or technology. We strive to continually improve the user experience for everyone, and we actively apply the relevant accessibility standards across our platform.

Our Commitment

We believe that everyone deserves equal access to information and services, including people with:

  • Visual impairments
  • Hearing impairments
  • Motor difficulties
  • Cognitive limitations
  • Color blindness or other visual differences

Measures We Take

To ensure accessibility, WinThrive implements the following strategies:

  • Use of proper semantic HTML for structure and clarity
  • Descriptive alt attributes on all informative images
  • Keyboard navigability throughout the website
  • High contrast colors for readability
  • Responsive design across screen sizes and assistive devices

Standards & Guidelines

Our website is built with reference to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, aiming for conformance level AA.

Third-Party Content

While we strive to maintain full accessibility throughout our platform, some external links or third-party elements may not fully comply with accessibility standards. We cannot guarantee the accessibility of content not hosted directly by WinThrive.
